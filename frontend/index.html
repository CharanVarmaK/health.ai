🔒 Privacy & Data Security

HealthAI takes your health data privacy seriously:

  • All personal health information (PHI) is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Sessions use short-lived JWT tokens with automatic rotation
  • Your data is never sold or shared with advertisers
  • You can export or permanently delete all your data at any time
  • Chat history is encrypted and only accessible to you
